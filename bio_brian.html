Brian Pelletier

  • Creative Director, Director Game Development

About Brian

Brian is a Creative director and art director with 20 years of extensive experience in developing video game design and artistic direction for high quality console and PC games. He is the creative director and head of game development for the combined Learning Games Network, Madison WI studio and Games + Learning + Society Center, applying his game development experience helping to create engaging and immersive games for learning and social Impact. Brian was instrumental in designing and developing games from inception to completion for more than 16 retail video games during 18 years with Raven Software (Activision) creating critical and financial hits. Additionally, Pelletier served as game director for three high profile projects and was responsible for art direction and aesthetic vision for eight highly acclaimed titles.
